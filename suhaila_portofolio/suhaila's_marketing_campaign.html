Insights & Optimization
What worked for
Odyssey.
Creative Angle

"Find your next movie in minutes" outperformed feature-heavy messaging and improved landing engagement across paid social placements.

Audience Fit

College and early-career viewers responded best to simple value framing: easy recommendations, quick swiping, and watchlist convenience.

Retention Trigger

Reminder emails with "3 new picks waiting" drove consistent return sessions and helped convert first-time users into repeat explorers.

Optimization Focus

Shortened onboarding copy and stronger social proof reduced friction and improved sign-up completion from trial audiences.
